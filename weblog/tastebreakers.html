Scatterbrain

by Colby Duke

Tastebreakers

04/03/21

Fireworks - Animal Collective. The sound of a tornado of nostalgia.
Dsco - Sweet Trip. A shoegaze banger and /mu classic.
Aphasia - Pinegrove. This track by a New Jersey-native alt-country band features some of the most vulnerable lyrics I have ever heard.
Str8 Outta Mumbai - Jai Paul. A song by an experimental artist so talented that he was heralded as the second coming of Prince before he went dark.
Fox - Dogleg. A single from an up-and-coming band whose lead guitarist tapes the words “Play fast” to the neck of his instrument at concerts.
